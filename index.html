Selected Experience & Projects

Siglec-7 recognition models (self & non-self glycans)

Integration of experimental and in silico data to define molecular recognition models for Siglec-7 with gangliosides and bacterial glycans. Development of computational pipelines for MD trajectory analysis, docking, and glycan conformational sampling.

Ion mobility native MS kinetics (Université de Genève)

Quantification of association/dissociation kinetics across 21 protein–ligand systems using Bruker timsTOF SCP (ion mobility MS). Data interrogation and interpretation of binding profiles.
